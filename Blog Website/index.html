Mountain Oasis

Welcome to Mountain Oasis, your gateway to awe-inspiring peaks and breathtaking landscapes. Whether you're a seasoned mountaineer or a first-time explorer, we’re here to guide and ignite your passion for the mountains. Discover hidden trails, conquer summits, and immerse in nature’s serenity.

Featured Mountains

Mount Everest

Mount Everest, the world's highest peak at 8,848.86 meters (29,031.7 feet), towers majestically in the Himalayas, straddling Nepal and Tibet. Known as "Sagarmatha" in Nepal and "Chomolungma" in Tibet, it attracts adventurers worldwide.

Kangchenjunga

Kangchenjunga, the world's third-highest peak at 8,586 meters (28,169 feet), lies on the border of Nepal and India in the Himalayas.Its remote location, stunning landscapes, and challenging routes make it a revered and awe-inspiring destination
